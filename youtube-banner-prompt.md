## Post-Generation Steps

1. **Generate** the banner at the highest resolution available
2. **Upscale** to exactly 2560 × 1440 px if needed
3. **Add text overlay** in Canva or Photoshop:
   - **"METRONAGON MEDIA"** — centered, large, gold serif font (Playfair Display or Cinzel), letter-spacing: 0.15em
   - **"THE MEASURE OF EXCELLENCE"** — smaller, below the main text, gold, tracked-out
   - **"Book Covers · Series Branding · Children's Books"** — subtle, small, below tagline
4. **Test** by uploading to YouTube Studio → Customization → Branding → Banner image
5. **Verify** the safe area (1546 × 423 px center) shows the logo and key text on mobile
