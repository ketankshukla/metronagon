Duration: 60 seconds
Purpose: Showcase the end-to-end capability — covers + branding + series architecture + children's books — all from one source. This is the "why hire five freelancers when you can hire one" pitch.

VISUAL DIRECTION

This script is structured as a STACK — each service layer is introduced visually and verbally, building on top of the previous one. Think of a Keynote presentation where each slide reveals another capability. The visual metaphor is building blocks assembling into a complete tower. By the end, the viewer sees the full scope of what Metronagon delivers and understands that no one else offers this complete package.
