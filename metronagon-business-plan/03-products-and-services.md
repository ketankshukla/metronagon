# Products & Services

## Service Categories

Metronagon Media offers four primary service categories, plus a suite of updates, revisions, and add-ons for existing clients. All services are digital — no physical goods are produced or shipped. Deliverables are transmitted via Google Drive or ZIP file.

---

**Scope clarification:** Front cover image only. Does not include spine or back cover design. Clients use Amazon Cover Creator or similar tools for full print wraps.

### Standard — $450

- **Delivery:** 3–5 business days
- **Includes:**
  - 1 front cover image (Kindle or paperback)
  - 1600×2400, 300 DPI — Amazon KDP-ready
  - Photorealistic or stylized art direction
  - 2 concept variations (two fully realized visual directions)
  - 1 polished final with professional typography and color correction
  - 1 round of revisions

### Premium — $650

- **Delivery:** 5–7 business days
- **Includes:**

## Post-Purchase Process

Every completed purchase triggers the following workflow:

1. **Payment confirmation** — Client pays securely via Stripe embedded checkout on metronagon.com
2. **Thank-you page** — Client receives a confirmation page with their specific package details, estimated delivery timeline, and a detailed requirements questionnaire customized to their purchased package
3. **Creative brief submission** — Client provides book details, visual direction, reference covers, and any special requirements via the questionnaire
4. **Production** — Metronagon begins work using the established creative pipeline
5. **Delivery** — High-resolution files delivered via Google Drive or ZIP within the stated timeframe
6. **Revisions** — Client reviews deliverables and requests adjustments within their included revision rounds
