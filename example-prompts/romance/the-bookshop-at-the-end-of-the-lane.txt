---

A charming, bright, illustrated book cover in the clean flat-design digital illustration style that has become the signature look of modern romantic comedy covers. The style is deliberately non-photorealistic: characters are stylized with simple geometric features, buildings are charmingly simplified, and every element is rendered as flat color shapes with clean edges and no texture. The overall effect is friendly, accessible, and inviting — the visual equivalent of a warm hug and a cup of tea.

THE SCENE — A QUAINT ENGLISH BOOKSHOP ON A SUNNY MORNING:

The cover depicts the exterior of a small, charming bookshop on a narrow English village lane, rendered in the simplified, slightly idealized style of flat-design illustration. The bookshop is the central element, filling approximately 30-80% of the image vertically.

THE BOOKSHOP:

A narrow two-story building with a traditional English shopfront — rendered in flat, cheerful colors with clean geometric shapes:
- The upper story is cream-colored (#FFF5E0) with two sash windows rendered as simple white rectangles with grey mullion lines. Window boxes overflow with stylized flowers — simple geometric shapes in coral pink (#FF7F7F), butter yellow (#FFE066), and lavender purple (#B088CC). The flowers are not botanically detailed — they are cheerful dots, circles, and simple petal shapes.
- The shopfront is painted in a rich teal green (#2A8B8B) with a large display window showing stacks of colorful books inside — each book a simple rectangle in a different cheerful color (coral, mint, yellow, sky blue, lavender). The shop door is a contrasting warm coral (#FF7F7F) with a simple brass doorknob rendered as a single gold circle.
- Above the door, a hanging wooden sign rendered as a simple rectangle with rounded corners in cream, containing the shop name "The Bookish Nook" in a friendly hand-lettered style in teal green. The sign hangs from a decorative iron bracket rendered as a simple curved line.
- A striped awning in cream and teal stripes extends over the shopfront — simple alternating color bands with a scalloped lower edge.
- On the pavement in front of the shop, a small outdoor book display: a simple wooden table (flat brown rectangle) with stacked books, and a sandwich board sign reading "OPEN" in cheerful hand-lettering.

THE TWO CHARACTERS:

Standing in front of the bookshop, two stylized figures interact in a charming moment:

THE WOMAN (shop owner): Standing in the open doorway, leaning against the door frame, holding a stack of books against her chest with one arm. She is rendered in the flat illustration style: a simple oval head with large expressive eyes (simple black dots with white highlights), a small smile, dark brown skin, and natural curly hair rendered as a rounded cloud shape in dark brown. She wears a simple A-line dress in butter yellow (#FFE066) with a cream cardigan, rendered as flat color shapes. Her body language is relaxed and amused — weight on one hip, head slightly tilted.

THE MAN (delivery person/love interest): Standing on the pavement, having just arrived, holding a large box of books. He is rendered in the same style: oval head, expressive dot eyes, light olive skin, short dark hair. He wears a simple navy blue (#2A3A5A) delivery uniform (flat color shapes). His body language communicates awkward charm — one hand scratching the back of his head, the other holding the box, a slightly embarrassed smile. He has clearly just said something funny or fumbling.

The characters face each other with the geometry of attraction — angled toward each other, the space between them charged with unspoken possibility. The flat illustration style makes the body language MORE expressive, not less — because the faces are simplified, the poses and gestures carry all the emotional weight.

OVERALL MOOD:

Warm, charming, irresistible — the feeling of stumbling upon a perfect little bookshop in a perfect little village and meeting someone who makes you want to stay. The flat illustration style is deliberately approachable — it signals "this is a fun, warm, easy read" without being childish. The bright palette and charming details (the cat, the bunting, the bicycle) create a world the reader wants to inhabit.

At thumbnail, the bright teal-and-coral bookshop facade with the two figures is immediately readable and genre-recognizable. At full resolution, the charming details — the cat in the window, the bunting, the awkward body language — reward a closer look.

THIS IS FLAT-DESIGN DIGITAL ILLUSTRATION. Clean, geometric, colorful, friendly. No texture, no photorealism, no gradients. The beauty is in the simplicity, the color harmony, and the way stylized figures can communicate more romantic chemistry through body language than photographic portraits can through facial expression.
